Process

How it works?

A four step simple process to reducing the stress of managing staff.

Sign Up

Simply register your business with QuickRoster in the mobile application or Online.

Add Staff

Register staff members, they will be emailed tempary login credentials so they can log in to the app.

Add shifts

Simply add the shifts for the staff members and they will be notified on their device and emailed a summary.

Relax

Relax. All the hard work is taken care of, if a staff member cannot make a shift, you will be notified by the app.

Features

Great features you'll love.

QuickRoster has a range of features that takes the hasstle and stress out of organising staff.

Shift Control

If a staff member cannot make a shift, they have to option to flag it which notifies the employer and offers it to other employees that arent working at that time.

Reporting

QuickRoster keeps track of the total weekly hours your buisness is using, and has graphs to display how many shifts each staff member is working, making payroll more efficent.

Notifications

No need to waste time calling, texting and emailing employees, let us take care of it for you by notifying them from the mobile app.

So Simple

QuickRoster has a minimilistic user interface that is so natrual to use. No learning is required.

Works with you

Employees shifts are automatically added into their devices calendar, so they can plan leasure outside of work.

Faq

Questions and Answers.

Here our are most commonly asked questions, if your questions not listed here, or you have something else to say, send us an email.

What are the security features?

All the data is stored on our Parse servers, and on its way to and from your device it is securely encrypted and decrypted so no one snooping can see your information.

How can I update staff profiles?

You can quickly update staff profiles by selecting 'Manage Staff' in the app or online.

What features are not included in the trial version?

The trial version includes all the features as the other plans.

Can I change plans?

Of course, just log in to the website and you can manage your plan under 'My Account'.

What if a staff member doesn't have the app?

They can either check there shifts on the web version, or they can read the summary of shifts emailed to them.

How long can I use the free trial version?

The trial version lasts for one month, after that it will automatically move you to the best suited plan for your buisness, you can cancel at anytime.
